Tour


Signing Up

Signing up is easy, click on sign up on the header, then either fill out your information or sign up with github!.

Joining A Project

To find a project, go to the projects page, adjust the filters for the language you want, the appropriate difficulty and availability and click "Code Together". You will then be prompted to send a message to the project's owner. That owner will get a notification, and can add you to the project (or let you know if it is full).

Editing User Settings

To edit your user settings, click on the user icon on the navigation bar. Once loaded you will find an edit button to change your password or your photo.

Editing Your Profile

To edit your profile, go to your dashboard, and click the Add link next to the corresponding section. To change your status click on the text under your avatar.

Editing Your Projects

To edit projects you are the owner of go to your dashboard, under a project you own, click on the details link. Make the changes you want and save, then close. All set!
