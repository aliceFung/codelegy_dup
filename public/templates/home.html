F.A.Q.

You have a question? Someone else might have had the exact same one. Lets find out!

Will you tech me code?

No, that's not what we are about, however, we will point you in the direction to head. And find other's at your skill level to learn with.

How much will this cost me?

Nothing! The site will always be free to find people to code with!

What if the language I want isn't an option?

There are two solutions for this, first, you can post in a related language if avaliable. If you want to pair with others for ReactJs, then post in the JavaScript category. Second if you feel that something should be added shoot us an email

How do I report bugs or pitch new ideas?

We are open to feedback, ideas, suggestions, volunteer work, just send a an email and we will get in touch
